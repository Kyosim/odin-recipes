Soy Marinated Egg

Description

Delicious boiled eggs marinated in a savoury soy sauce broth

This is a common dish enjoyed by Koreans for its simplicity and salty flavor! You can have the eggs on their own with a bowl of rice, or combine them with some other side dishes to your liking. Versatility is a real pro for this dish! As long as you have the patience to wait for the eggs to really soak in the soy sauce, you'll have a great meal ready for you anytime you're hungry.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs (however many you like)
  • Vinegar
  • Ginger and Garlic (optional)
